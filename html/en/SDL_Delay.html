NAME
       SDL_Delay - Wait a specified number of milliseconds before returning.

SYNOPSIS
       #include "SDL.h"

       void SDL_Delay(Uint32 ms);

DESCRIPTION
       Wait  a specified number of milliseconds before returning. SDL_Delay will
       wait at least the specified time, but possible longer due to OS  schedul‐
       ing.

              Note:

              Count  on  a  delay  granularity of at least 10 ms. Some platforms
              have shorter clock ticks but this is the most common.
