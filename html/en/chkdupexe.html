NAME
       chkdupexe - find duplicate executables

SYNOPSIS
       chkdupexe

DESCRIPTION
       chkdupexe  will  scan  the  union of $PATH and a hardcoded list of common
       locations for binaries.  It will  report  dangling  symlinks  and  dupli‐
       cately-named binaries.
