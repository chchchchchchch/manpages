NAME
       gnome-sound-recorder - simple sound recorder

SYNOPSIS
       gnome-sound-recorder [OPTION...]

DESCRIPTION
       gnome-sound-recorder enables you to record and play .flac, .ogg, and .wav
       sound files.

OPTIONS
       gnome-sound-recorder only accepts the standard GNOME and GTK options.

AUTHORS
       gnome-sound-recorder was written by Iain  Holmes  <iain@prettypeople.org>
       and others.

       This  manual  page  was  written  by Sven Arvidsson <sa@whiz.se>, for the
       Debian project (but may be used by others).
