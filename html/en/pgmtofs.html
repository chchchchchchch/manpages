NAME
       pgmtofs - convert portable graymap to Usenix FaceSaver(tm) format

SYNOPSIS
       pgmtofs [pgmfile]

DESCRIPTION
       Reads  a portable graymap as input.  Produces Usenix FaceSaver(tm) format
       as output.

       FaceSaver is a registered trademark of Metron Computerware Ltd.  of  Oak‐
       land, CA.

       fstopgm(1), pgm(5)
