NAME
       pnminvert - invert a portable anymap

SYNOPSIS
       pnminvert [pnmfile]

DESCRIPTION
       Reads  a  portable  anymap as input.  Inverts it black for white and pro‐
       duces a portable anymap as output.

SEE ALSO
       pnm(5)
